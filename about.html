ABOUT IEEE CSUN

Build with community. Grow with industry.

IEEE CSUN is a student organization that helps members develop real technical skills through hands-on workshops, project nights, and professional development.

Whether you’re exploring electronics, software, cybersecurity, embedded systems, or career prep — we create a place to learn fast, meet people, and ship projects you can proudly put on your resume.

  • Workshops: practical skills (tools, resume, interviews, tech topics)
  • Projects: team builds that turn into portfolio pieces
  • Networking: alumni + industry speakers + collaborations
  • Leadership: committees and board opportunities
