ABOUT IEEE CSUN

Build skills. Build community.

IEEE CSUN is the student branch of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers at California State University, Northridge.

We focus on hands-on workshops, project-based learning, and professional development to help students grow technically and professionally.

  • Workshops: technical & career-focused
  • Projects: team-based portfolio builds
  • Networking: alumni & industry speakers
  • Leadership: committees & officer roles
